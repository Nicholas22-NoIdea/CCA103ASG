About This Scholarship

The Excellence Scholarship Program is designed to recognize and support academically outstanding students who demonstrate exceptional potential and commitment to their studies. This merit-based scholarship provides financial support to eligible undergraduate and postgraduate students.

Key Benefits

  • Annual cash award of RM 15,000
  • Renewable for up to 4 years (subject to academic performance)
  • Mentorship program with senior university officials
  • Priority access to internship opportunities
  • Recognition in university publications
  • Invitation to exclusive networking events
Eligibility Criteria
Minimum CGPA: 3.50 or above
Academic Status: Full-time student in good academic standing
Citizenship: Malaysian citizen or permanent resident
Program: Undergraduate or Postgraduate (all faculties)
No Pending Dues: No outstanding fees or library fines
Required Documents
  • Completed application form
  • Official academic transcript (latest semester)
  • Copy of national ID or passport
  • Letter of recommendation from faculty
  • Personal statement (500-750 words)
  • Proof of citizenship (birth certificate or PR certificate)
  • Bank account details for direct transfer
