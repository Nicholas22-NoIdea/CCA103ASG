User Roles & Access

👨‍🎓

Student

The primary user of the application portal. Students can search for scholarships, submit applications, upload supporting documents, and track their application status in real-time.

Access to:
  • Personal Dashboard
  • Scholarship Directory
  • Application Forms
  • Document Upload
  • Status Tracking
  • Application History
⚙️

Administrator

System managers who handle scholarship configuration, eligibility rule definition, and application reviews. They manage the operational aspects of the scholarship program.

Access to:
  • Admin Dashboard
  • Scholarship Management
  • Rule Configuration
  • Eligibility Screening
  • Document Verification
  • Application Review
  • Award Allocation
⭐

Evaluator / Interview Committee

Evaluation specialists who assess candidates through interviews and scoring. They manage the evaluation workflow and contribute to merit-based rankings.

Access to:
  • Evaluation Dashboard
  • Candidate Profiles
  • Interview Scoring
  • Ranking Reviews
  • Analytics
  • Weight Configuration
💰

Finance & Awards Officer

Financial professionals responsible for award letter generation, bank verification, and payout processing. They ensure accurate and timely disbursement of awards.

Access to:
  • Award Dashboard
  • Payout Management
  • Bank Verification
  • Award Letters
  • Eligibility Verification
  • Reports
👔

University Leadership

Executive decision-makers who need high-level insights into the scholarship program. They access reports and analytics for strategic planning and oversight.

Access to:
  • Dashboards & Reports
  • Analytics & Insights
  • Program Statistics
  • Export Reports
  • Historical Data

Core Workflows

1. Student Application Workflow

1
Authentication

Student logs in to personal dashboard

2
Discovery

Search and filter scholarships by type, amount, and deadline

3
Review Details

View scholarship requirements and eligibility criteria

4
Apply

Complete multi-step application form with all required information

5
Upload Documents

Submit supporting documents (transcript, ID, income proof, etc.)

6
Track Status

Monitor application progress through timeline and notifications

2. Admin Review & Approval Workflow

1
Receive Applications

View all submitted applications in admin dashboard

2
Automated Screening

Run eligibility checks against configured criteria

3
Document Verification

Review and verify submitted documents

4
Manual Review

Assess application completeness and quality

5
Shortlist

Identify qualified candidates for further evaluation

3. Evaluation & Ranking Workflow

1
Schedule Interviews

Set interview dates and times for shortlisted candidates

2
Conduct Interviews

Meet with candidates and assess their suitability

3
Record Scores

Input scores for academic, interview, and motivation criteria

4
Generate Rankings

Apply weightage and generate merit-based rankings

5
Review & Approve

Leadership approval of final rankings and award recommendations

4. Award & Payout Workflow

1
Finalize Awards

Lock in final award decisions and amounts

2
Generate Award Letters

Create official award letters in PDF format

3
Collect Bank Details

Request and verify student bank account information

4
Process Payouts

Execute fund transfers to verified bank accounts

5
Ongoing Monitoring

Verify continued eligibility (CGPA, enrollment status)

5. Reporting & Analytics Workflow

1
Generate Reports

Create scholarship, eligibility, ranking, and payout reports

2
Filter & Search

Apply date ranges and search criteria to data

3
View Analytics

Analyze trends, statistics, and performance metrics

4
Export Data

Export reports as PDF or Excel for further analysis
